Our Story

Melti began with a mother’s love — and grew into a mission.

My daughter is a model who adores desserts, yet her work often meant saying no to sweets. As a mother and a baker, I wanted her to enjoy the pleasure of dessert without the burden of guilt.

That’s how Melti was born — with a promise: to make desserts that are as kind to the body as they are joyful to the heart.

But this love didn’t stop at my daughter. I realized there are so many girls and women — just like her — who crave sweetness, beauty, and indulgence, but are held back by health concerns or fear of gaining weight.

At Melti, every recipe is carefully crafted with reduced sugar, balanced ingredients, and a fresh, light texture — all without compromising on taste or elegance.

Because we believe dessert should never be a source of stress. It should be a celebration — of beauty, of balance, and of joy.

Melti is where indulgence meets intention — and every bite melts with care.Welcome to Melti — let’s make life a little sweeter.
